--- layout: project category: projects title: CS247 - Trianguletter ---

TrianguLetter is an interactive, table-top, word-building game. Kids throw and move tiles to capture moving letters, which they can use to form new words and learn their definitions. The more complicated the word, the more points the kids get.

  • Designed the graphics and the physical interaction
  • Technical challenges included projection, motion-tracking, calibration, and effective use of a tabletop as a medium for gameplay
  • Collaborated with 5th-grade students from a local school
  • It was an exciting challenge to transform a computer game into tile-throwing, letter-capturing fun!
